Case Study

Background

NIKO, a software developer from Africa, left his job at an IT firm in 2022 to start freelancing. His skills included JavaScript, React,pyhon,django and Node.js.

Challenge

  • Finding consistent clients
  • Managing income flow
  • Handling projects independently

Approach

  • Built a strong LinkedIn and GitHub profile
  • Created a personal portfolio website
  • Used platforms like Upwork and Freelancer.com
  • Organized work with Trello, Slack, and Notion

Outcome

  • 15+ international clients in one year
  • Income doubled by year-end
  • Secured long-term contracts with 3 clients
Key Insight: Success in tech freelancing relies on showing your work, marketing yourself, and strong communication.
